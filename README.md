# 🚲 Bike Purchase Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The **Bike Purchase Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior related to bike purchases. It provides insights into how factors such as gender, region, education, marital status, and occupation influence bike-buying decisions.

The dashboard helps businesses identify key customer segments and make data-driven marketing and sales decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze bike purchase patterns across different customer groups.
- Identify the regions with the highest bike purchases.
- Compare bike purchases by gender and marital status.
- Understand the impact of education level on bike purchasing behavior.
- Provide interactive filtering for deeper analysis.

---

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 1. Bike Purchased by Gender
- Male customers purchased **525 bikes**.
- Female customers purchased **501 bikes**.
- Male customers show a slightly higher purchase rate.

### 2. Bike Purchased by Region
- **North America** has the highest number of bike purchases (**508**).
- **Europe** follows with **316** purchases.
- **Pacific** region recorded **202** purchases.

### 3. Bike Purchased by Education
- **Bachelors** degree holders purchased the most bikes (**311**).
- **Partial College** customers are the second-largest buyer group (**278**).
- **Partial High School** customers recorded the lowest purchases (**78**).

### 4. Bike Purchased by Marital Status
- **Married customers (54%)** purchased more bikes than **Single customers (46%)**.
- Marital status appears to influence purchasing decisions.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Focus marketing campaigns on **North America**, as it has the highest purchase volume.
2. Target customers with **Bachelor's and Partial College education**, as they represent the largest buyer segments.
